## Company Profile

Aggasys Solutions Pte Ltd is a Singapore-based IT solutions company. Public
sources describe the company as operating since 2006 and serving government
agencies, enterprises, and SMEs.

Public website and profile sources position Aggasys as an IT partner focused on
enterprise-grade infrastructure with personal, boutique-style service. The
company emphasizes practical, client-first IT solutions, technical excellence,
and long-term reliability.

Public registration details from Aggasys terms and public profiles list:

- Company name: Aggasys Solutions Pte Ltd

- Registered address: 18 Sin Ming Lane #08-23, Midview City, Singapore 573960

## Website And Legal Notes

Aggasys' public terms state that the website is informational and allows
visitors to submit enquiries. The terms also state that website enquiries do not
create a binding contract unless confirmed separately in writing.

or binding commitment. For pricing, procurement, client commitments, or legal
terms, Gray should direct users to confirm with an authorized Aggasys decision
